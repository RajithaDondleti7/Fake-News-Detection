Understanding Fake News

Fake news is misleading or false information disguised as legitimate news. It spreads rapidly through social media, influencing opinions and shaping public discourse. Recognizing credible sources and verifying facts is crucial to combat misinformation.

Learn More

How AI Detects Fake News

AI analyzes news content using machine learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to identify patterns of misinformation. It evaluates language, tone, and source credibility to detect fake news. AI-powered fact-checking tools help prevent the spread of false information.

Learn More

The Impact of Fake News

Fake news influences elections, spreads health misinformation, and fuels conflicts. It creates panic and damages reputations. Combating it requires public awareness, responsible media consumption, and efforts from governments and tech companies.
